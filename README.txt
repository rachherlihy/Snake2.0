-- DESCRIPTION -- 
Snake2.0 is a hybrid of the classic game Snake and Agar.io

-- INSTRUCTIONS --
Play as the Snake (circle object) and eat your way through the food (square moving objects) in sequential
order, from smallest to highest (1-24). Eating or hitting into food objects out of order will result in the
snakes death while eating the smallest available food object will add segments to the snakes body and remove
the food object. Hitting the walls of the game frame will also result in the snakes death.

-- CONTROLS --
W - up
A - left
S - down
D - right

Right-hand side panel give options for speed and restarting the game

-- Play --
Executable file: dist > Snake2.0.jar
